Ingredients

  • Thick Pork Chop 230g
  • Shredded Cabbage 100g
  • Dark, rich thick sauce(3 Tbsp ponzu, 3Tbsp sake, 1/2 Tbsp soy sauce)
  • 1 Tbsp All Purpose Flour (aka Plain Flour)
  • Black Pepper and Salt both 1/8 Tbsp(Optional)
  • Garlic

Serve with

  • White rice
  • Miso soup

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients, 1 small bowl and 1 medium bowl
  2. Gather all the ingredients
  3. Over a small bowl, 1/4 onion (use about half of it) and set aside
  4. Shred the cabbage into thin slice
  5. In medium bowl, combine ponzu, sake , soy sauce and whisk all together for the sauce
  6. In order to sear the perfectly, pat dry the pork chop with paper towels. Removing the moisture will prevent the meat from being steamed
  7. Make pork being Glove-cut
  8. Colt the meat with flour and remove excess flour
  9. Put the lid on and fry for 3 minutes on a medium heat
  10. After 3 minutes, turn down the heat and flip the pork chops over. Continue to cook for 3 minutes, agin with the lid on
  11. Transfer pork to a plate
  12. In the same pan, add the ingredients of sauce
  13. Put them on the same plate
  14. Drizzle the sauce from the pan
  15. Bon Appetit
